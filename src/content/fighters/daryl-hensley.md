Daryl Hensley is a fan favorite despite—or perhaps because of—his losing record. A maintenance worker at a state park in the Ozarks, Hensley competes in the KPL "for the extra income and because my wife says I need a hobby." He is the oldest active fighter on the heavyweight roster and holds the KPL record for most rounds fought (247) and most rounds absorbed without producing blood (189). He has never once appeared concerned during a bout.
