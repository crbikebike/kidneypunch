Buck McCrae is the KPL's most marketable fighter and its first homegrown star. A former junior rodeo champion and pipeline welder, McCrae discovered kidney punching at a bar in Midland, Texas, where it was apparently a regular Friday night activity. He joined the KPL in 2020 and has held the Heavyweight title twice. His sponsorship deal with Patriot Beef Jerky is the largest individual endorsement in KPL history.
