Viktor Dragovic grew up in rural Serbia, where kidney strikes were reportedly a common feature of village festivals. He came to the KPL in 2021 after a viral video of him splitting a watermelon with an open-handed body blow garnered 14 million views on ToughTube. Known for his stoic demeanor and near-superhuman pain tolerance, Dragovic has never been stopped by Hematuria Stoppage in 56 professional bouts.
